Privacy Policy of AutoCheckDoc

Effective Date: June 11, 2025

1. Personal Data

AutoCheckDoc does not collect, store, or share personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, etc.

2. Stored Data

The application stores your license plate number, vignette, insurance, technical inspection, and notification data locally on your device. This data is not sent to external servers.

3. Notifications

AutoCheckDoc uses local notifications. The user can deny or modify permissions from the device's settings.

4. Third Parties

The application does not use third-party services and does not show any ads.

5. Security

The data is kept exclusively on your device and is erased upon uninstallation. If you use iCloud Backup, the data may be backed up through it.

6. Changes

You will be notified of any policy changes through the App Store or a new version of the application.

7. Contact

If you have any questions, you can contact us at: autocheckdoc@gmail.com
